Spatial Navigation Task

Sign Language & Spatial Cognition Research

Study Information

Duration: Approximately 20 minutes
Trials: 135 trials across 9 blocks
Task Types: Player view & Map view navigation
Practice: 4 practice trials included

⚠️ Requirements

  • Computer with keyboard (arrow keys required)
  • Stable internet connection
  • Quiet environment without distractions
  • Chrome or Firefox browser recommended
Begin Experiment

What You'll Do

Navigate through simple mazes using two different strategies:

  • Player View: Navigate as if you are the player in the maze
  • Map View: Navigate using compass directions

Your responses help us understand how sign language experience affects spatial thinking.

For technical support or questions, contact the research team at action.brain.lab@gallaudet.edu.

Your data is automatically saved and kept confidential.
Participant IDs are anonymized for privacy.
